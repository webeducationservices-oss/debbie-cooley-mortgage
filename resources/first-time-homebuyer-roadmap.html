What's inside

  1. Are you a first-time buyer? The federal definition is broader than most people realize. You may qualify even if you've owned before.
  2. What credit score you actually need — including how to improve a score 20–60 points within 30–60 days.
  3. Florida down payment assistance — Hometown Heroes ($35K), Florida Bond, and county programs. How to layer them.
  4. Choosing the right loan type — FHA vs. conventional vs. VA vs. USDA, with concrete guidance on which fits which situation.
  5. The complete documents checklist — every document you'll need to assemble before pre-approval.
  6. What happens at every step from pre-approval through underwriting to closing day.
  7. Your next steps — how to schedule a free consultation if you want personalized guidance.

Who this guide is for

This guide was written for first-time homebuyers in Pasco, Pinellas, and Hillsborough Counties (the Tampa Bay region of Florida). The principles apply to most Florida buyers — but the down payment assistance programs, county-specific resources, and price-range examples are tailored to West Florida.
